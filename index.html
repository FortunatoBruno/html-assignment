About me.

One of the things I am most proud of having learned from my mother's teachings is to never give up. She has always encouraged us - me and my two brothers - to never give up, especially when it comes to making our dreams come true. And I say dreams instead of objective because it has a more subjective connotation. This has much more to do with personal fulfillment than with being successful in society, although one does not exclude the other.

So, I decided to leave my whole life behind in Brazil and come to Canada seeking to fulfill my dream of becoming a web designer and building a future here, and it is not the stones in the long road that will be able to make me give up to do it.

"The greatest lessons I have every learned were at my mother's knees... All that I am, or hope to be, I owe to my angel mother." - Abraham Lincoln
